What is Cameo?

Cameo is an innovative camera app for both iOS and Android and lets you capture the best moments in life either on still photos or videos. Cameo have many useful features to enhance the photos and videos with editing tools, filters and effects. And when you're done you can easily share the result with all your friends and the rest of the world through Instagram.

Cameo has been designed from the botton-up by photography experts and award-winning app developers to make sure that you get the best camera app ever created. We are continuously improving the app with new features - this is just the beggining.
